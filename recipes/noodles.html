Noodles

Something almost every kid loves. Something almost every insane person loves. The instant noodle in a take I have no clue if it'll actually ever be tasty.

Ingredients

Recipe yields 1 servings

For more servings, open more noodlepacks.

  • 1 pack of your favourite instant noodle

Instructions

  1. Crack the pack open
  2. Fill your designated eating bowl with water
  3. Put the water from your bowl to your pot and boil it
  4. When the water is boiling, insert the noodles carefully to avoid splashing yourself with hot water
  5. Read the package for how long to cook it, usually 2 minutes.
  6. Add flavouring to your bowl of choice
  7. Pour the noodles over to your bowl with the water and stir the flavouring to life.

  8. EAT
