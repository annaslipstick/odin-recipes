Meatballs

If Sweden should be remembered by one thing, and only one thing it should be meatballs. The brown, juicy, balls of meat. With proper seasoning and onion it'll be a perfect dish to enjoy a cold winter night.

Ingredients

Recipe yields 12 servings

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • ½ cup bread crumbs
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons grated onion
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C)
  2. Mix ground beef, bread crumbs, egg, onion, black pepper, nutmeg, and salt together in a bowl
  3. Form into small balls; arrange balls on a baking sheet
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes. Flip meatballs and continue baking until cooked through, 12 to 15 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C)
